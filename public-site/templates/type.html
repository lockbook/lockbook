Icons

Line icons treated as glyphs — same family as the letterforms. Sized to text, stroked at 1.5, rendered in currentColor so they inherit the surrounding text color and any state on its parent. Muted at rest, ink on hover when interactive.

Brand tone

dense · easy · artful

Lockbook talks like a thoughtful person, not like a product. Quiet confidence, concrete claims, warm cadence. Every sentence passes three filters before it ships.

The test

Dense

no words wasted

If you can cut it, cut it. Compression is craft.

Easy

no re-reading

Ideas stay simple. One thought per sentence.

Artful

cadence lands

Beautifully simple, not clever. A line you'd remember.

In practice

Four patterns recur: one claim per sentence, plain words over jargon, numbers before morals, situation then exits. The specimens that follow put them in product context.
